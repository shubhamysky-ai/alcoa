About Alcoa Construction

Alcoa Construction is a reliable construction company based in St. George, Utah, dedicated to building high-quality homes and transforming residential spaces through professional construction and remodeling services.

Our team of experienced contractors, architects, and builders works closely with clients to deliver projects that combine functionality, durability, and modern design. From custom homes to kitchen and bathroom renovations, we handle every project with precision and care.

We believe every home should reflect the vision and lifestyle of its owner. That is why we focus on delivering personalized construction solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
